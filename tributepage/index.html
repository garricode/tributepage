Leonardo da Vinci

Timeline

  • 1452 - Born on April 15 in Vinci.
  • 1467 - Apprenticed to Andrea del Verrocchi in Florence.
  • 1472 - Becomes a member of the painter's guild in Florence.
  • 1495 - Begins painting the Last Supper in Milan.
  • 1502 - Becomes Cesare Borgia's military advisor.
  • 1516 - Permanently leaves Italy for France.
  • 1519 - Dies on May 2 at Cloux, France.

“One can have no smaller or greater mastery than mastery of oneself.”

- Leonardo da Vinci
